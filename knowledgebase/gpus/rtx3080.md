## Specs
- GPU: GA102, 10 GB GDDR6X (12GB variant exists), 320-bit bus
- CUDA Cores: 8704, Tensor Cores: 272 (3rd gen)
- FP16 TC: 59.8 TFLOPS, INT8: 119.5 TOPS
- L2 Cache: 5 MB, TDP: 320W
- Bandwidth: 760 GB/s (10GB) or 912 GB/s (12GB)

## Best Use
- Good for 7B models in INT4-INT8
- 12GB variant preferred for flexibility
