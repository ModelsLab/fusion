## Strengths

- very strong tensor throughput for single-GPU inference
- good fit for Triton attention and fused MLP kernels
- often benefits from graph capture for low-latency decode

## Constraints

- decode remains memory-sensitive because VRAM bandwidth is far below H100/B200
- Ada FP8 tensor cores support FP8 inference (vLLM, SGLang) but Transformer Engine FP8 training workflows are less mature than Hopper
