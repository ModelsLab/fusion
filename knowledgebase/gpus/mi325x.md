## Specs
- Architecture: CDNA 3 (same as MI300X with HBM3e)
- Memory: 256 GB HBM3e, 6 TB/s bandwidth
- Compute: same as MI300X (1307 FP16 TFLOPS)
- TDP: ~750W

## Key Advantage
256 GB can fit 405B models in INT4 on a single chip.
