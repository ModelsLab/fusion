## Specs
- GPU: GA104, 8 GB GDDR6, 256-bit bus
- CUDA Cores: 5888, Tensor Cores: 184 (3rd gen)
- FP16 TC: 40.6 TFLOPS, INT8: 81.3 TOPS
- L2 Cache: 4 MB, TDP: 220W

## Best Use
- Limited by 8GB VRAM - use for small models or heavily quantized
- 7B models need Q3_K or Q4_0 quantization to fit
