## Specs
- GPU: GA106, 12 GB GDDR6, 192-bit bus
- CUDA Cores: 3584, Tensor Cores: 112 (3rd gen)
- FP16 TC: 25.4 TFLOPS, INT8: 50.7 TOPS
- L2 Cache: 3 MB, Shared Memory/SM: 100 KB
- TDP: 170W, PCIe Gen 4 x16

## Best Use
- Run 7B models in INT4 (AWQ/GPTQ) for development
- Fine-tuning small models with QLoRA
- Not suitable for production serving
