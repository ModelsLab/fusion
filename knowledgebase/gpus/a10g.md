## Specs
- GPU: GA102-based, 24 GB GDDR6, 384-bit bus
- CUDA Cores: 9216, Tensor Cores: 288 (3rd gen)
- FP16 TC: 70.6 TFLOPS, INT8: 141.2 TOPS
- L2 Cache: 6 MB, TDP: 150W
- Widely available on AWS as g5 instances

## Best Use
- Cost-effective inference for 7B-13B models
- AWS g5 instances for production serving
- INT4/INT8 quantization recommended for throughput
